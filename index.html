What many don't know

From the first concert or a few 10,000 streams on Spotify & Co., GEMA membership is worth it for
you! We support you in protecting your copyrights. You retain full control of your songs and receive
compensation for their public use. Even small performances with your own songs are worthwhile.

We are the Robin Hood
on the hunt for your
royalties

A music publisher is like a tax advisor for musicians - but better! We register your works with GEMA, check invoices and actively search for missing royalties.

This gives you more time to concentrate on what you love: making music!
